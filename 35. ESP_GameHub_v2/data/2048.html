2048

How to Play
  • Use the arrow keys or the on-screen buttons to slide all tiles in that direction.
  • Matching tiles merge into one with double the value (e.g. 2+2→4).
  • After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot.
  • Reach the 2048 tile to win—and keep going for higher scores!
  • You can also swipe on mobile.
